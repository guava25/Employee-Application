IMPORTANT INFORMATION FOR APPLICANTS REGARDING

FINGERPRINTING BACKGROUND CHECKS

On January 27, 1997, Senate Bill # 160 became effective. This bill (section 3701.881) requires that fingerprinting background checks be performed on all potential applicants. There are two different kinds of background check. An Ohio background check may be performed if an individual can prove that he/she has been a resident of Ohio for 5 years. If a potential applicant can not submit substantial proof for 5 year Ohio residency, then an FBI background check along with an Ohio background check is required.
