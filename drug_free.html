DRUG-FREE WORKPLACE PROGRAM

In accordance with our company's Drug-Free Workplace (HR Policy) and Federal and State law, all employees as a condition of employment must:

  • Abide by the terms of the Drug-Free Workplace program
  • Notify the employer of any criminal drug statute conviction for a violation occurring in the workplace no later then five (5) days after such a conviction.

Within thirty (30) days of receiving notice of an employee's conviction, our company will impose remedial measures on the employee convicted of drug abuse violations in the workplace. Remedial action taken against the employee can be up to and including termination.
