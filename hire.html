Hiring Procedure & Conditions of Employment

All applicants must complete an application packet for the appropriate company to be considered for employment. Applicants for direct care positions must fill out an application for both Robland Home Health Care Corporation

Background Check: In order to be employed and retain employment, employees must be able to pass a Department of Human Services (DHS) background check. In order to provide direct care services for our clients, applicants' background check(s) must come back with a Done-Passed status (blue letter from DHS). Employees cannot begin working and will not be paid until all training and background checks are complete. Your start date must be approved and communicated to your by your direct supervisor. Background checks usually take between 5-10 business days to come back but can take longer.

If at any time during employment, DHS disqualifies the employee from providing direct care, the employee will be suspended. If the disqualification becomes final, the employee will be terminated.

Direct care workers (PCA, HHA, Homemaker,) will attend unpaid orientation in the office and must complete the following prior to beginning work:

  • Watch the following training videos: Intro to Homecare, Communication, HIPAA, Boundaries, and the Patient Cares Training Video.
  • Complete Infection Control and Fraud, Waste, and Abuse Training

Home Health Aides workers must have the training listed above as well as a Monteux (tuberculosis) test at time of hire and every 2 years thereafter, competency skill test in-person with an Robland RN, a 50questions HHA exam, and 12-lnservice hours of training per year through our RCTC Learn training website.

Driver's License/ Driving Record Check: All Office staff, Robland employees, Nurses, must have a current and valid driver's license. This is mandatory and employees must be insurable by our company's auto insurer to be authorized to drive our company vehicles. A current copy of applicant's driver's license is required. A copy of applicant's current, individual auto insurance card is required in order to provide support for insurability.

Be aware that additional Conditions of Employment may and do exist in our company policy manual and are subject to change as management deems necessary.

Important: All employees must turn their time cards in by noon on payroll Monday in order to be for the scheduled payroll (see payroll calendar for schedule). Any late timesheets will be processed on the next following payroll.
